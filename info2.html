Deforestation, AKA logging/clearcutting, is one of the major causes of climate change. The people involved in these activities burn a large section of trees away, and clear the remains. There are 3 main problems with this, 1 we have less oxygen to breathe because the fires burn oxygen and produce C02. 2nd it kills trees which slowly convert C02 back into oxygen, and 3rd destroy many animals habitats in the process.
